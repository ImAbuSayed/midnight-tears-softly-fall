[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
When the midnight tears softly fall on the glass,
I feel the heat of your body close to mine.
Watching the slow and the quiet hours pass,
Tasting your lips like they’re blackberry wine.

[Verse 1]
The room is dark and the town is fast asleep,
I can feel your breath across my collarbone.
This is the kind of secret I want to keep,
Making sure you never wake up here alone.

[Pre-Chorus]
The temperature is rising as we take it slow,
There's a gentle rhythm in the way you sway.
We’ve got nowhere else in the world to go,
Just letting every shadow melt away.

[Chorus]
Oh, the midnight tears softly fall on the glass,
Washing away everything but you and me.
I’m pulling you closer and making it last,
Drowning in the deep end of your endless sea.

[Post-Chorus]
(Midnight tears softly fall)
I want to hold you through the endless night.

[Verse 2]
Your fingers are tracing lines upon my skin,
And every little touch is a spark of fire.
I don't even know where you and I begin,
Caught up in the middle of this sweet desire.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The temperature is rising as we take it slow,
There's a gentle rhythm in the way you sway.
We’ve got nowhere else in the world to go,
Just letting every shadow melt away.

[Chorus]
Oh, the midnight tears softly fall on the glass,
Washing away everything but you and me.
I’m pulling you closer and making it last,
Drowning in the deep end of your endless sea.

[Post-Chorus 2]
(Midnight tears softly fall)
I want to hold you through the endless night.

[Bridge]
We can leave the heavy weight of the world behind,
Lock the door and let our true emotions show.
Every single heartbeat is perfectly aligned,
With the passion in this dim and tender glow.
I’m laying all my cards out for you to see,
There is nowhere else Saeed would rather be.
